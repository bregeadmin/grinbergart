— rule 01

always lowercase.

except proper nouns. menu boards, room numbers, the welcome card — all lower.

— rule 02

we, never i.

two people behind the desk. always "we did", "we wrote", "we cleaned the room". in russian — на ты.

— rule 03

one italic per line.

italic + orange picks the word that carries the meaning. one per headline, two per paragraph max.

— rule 04

numbers in numerals.

"room 12", not "room twelve". phone in international format.

— rule 05

say what you mean.

"the smallest one." "no parking." stating limits is louder than promising everything.

— rule 06

no emoji, ever.

printed materials and physical signage in particular. emoji on a sign reads like a deal site.

compact mark · b.

just the b + the dot. used wherever the full "bre." won't fit — the brass key tag, the favicon, telegram avatar, a corner stamp, embroidered on a towel, branded on a coaster.
